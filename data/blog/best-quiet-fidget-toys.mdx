## Buying Guide: How to Choose the Right Quiet Fidget

Before you buy, check these three factors to ensure you don't end up with a drawer full of unused plastic:

1.  **The Library Test:** If you use it in a dead-silent library, will the person next to you hear it? If the answer is "maybe," it’s not for the office.
2.  **One Hand vs. Two Hands:** Do you need to type while fidgeting? If so, avoid the Infinity Cube or Stretchy Strings. Go for **Calm Strips** or a **Fidget Ring**.
3.  **Visual Distraction:** For school, avoid light-up toys or anything too large. Small, matte-colored items like the **Marble Mesh** attract less attention from teachers.
